Introduction to Human Rights

Human rights are the basic rights and freedoms that belong to every person in the world, regardless of nationality, sex, ethnicity, language, religion, or any other status.

include the right to life, liberty, equality, and the right to participate in the cultural, social, and political life of the society

These rights are fundamental to human dignity and are protected by law at the national and international levels.

Throughout history, various documents have played a crucial role in shaping and advancing the concept of human rights.
