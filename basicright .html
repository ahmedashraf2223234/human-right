Basic Human Rights

Human rights include a wide range of civil, political, economic, social and cultural rights:

Right to Life and Liberty

Everyone has the right to life, liberty and security of person. No one shall be held in slavery or servitude.

right to Freedom

Everyone has the right to freedom of opinion and expression; this right includes freedom to hold opinions without interference.

Right to Education

Everyone has the right to education. Education shall be free, at least in the elementary and fundamental stages.

Right to Work

Everyone has the right to work, to free choice of employment, to just and favorable conditions of work.

Right to Equality

All are equal before the law and are entitled without any discrimination to equal protection of the law.

Right to Political Participation

Everyone has the right to take part in the government of their country, directly or through freely chosen representatives.

right to health

everyone deserves access to healthcare and a safe environment.
